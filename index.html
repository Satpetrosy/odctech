About Us

ODC tecnology LLC was founded in 2015 in the US and Armenia. The company was formed by experienced and highly motivated professionals from both countries based on a long-standing successful collaboration of the management and engineering teams providing high quality development and quality assurance services in the areas of software, cloud and electronic design automation products at a fraction of first world engineering costs.
